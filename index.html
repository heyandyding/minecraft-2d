2D Survival Prototype

Blockfall Night

Explore an even wider world with lakes, rivers, and villages, build shelter, craft gear, and survive a full Minecraft-style mob roster.

Wider World Water + Swimming Villages + Houses Hostile + Passive Mobs Building + Hunger

Controls

  • A / D move
  • W / Space jump or swim upward
  • Left click mine or attack mobs
  • Right click open chests or place selected block
  • 1-9 choose block or tool
  • H eat 1 food
  • E open or close crafting
  • Left click recipe craft it from the recipe panel
  • R respawn after death at a fresh spawn point

Goal

Chop trees, mine stone, swim through water, raid village supplies, place blocks, craft your table and wooden tools, and build a safe base before zombies, creepers, skeletons, spiders, slimes, and endermen reach you. Hunt animals and fish for food before starvation starts doing damage.

Survival

  • Water: slows falling and lets you swim up
  • Villages: villagers now roam inside village bounds
  • Animals: cows, pigs, sheep, and chickens wander the surface
  • Night mobs: zombies, creepers, skeletons, spiders, slimes, and endermen
  • Creepers: blasts damage terrain, but never remove water tiles
  • Food: fills 1 hunger bar when eaten
  • Hand crafting: planks, sticks, and crafting tables
  • Table crafting: wooden tools, furnaces, and chests
  • Death: each death rolls a fresh spawn point before respawn
